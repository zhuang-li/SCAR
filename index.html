ACL 2025 – SCAR: Data Selection via Style Consistency‑Aware Response Ranking for Efficient Instruction Tuning of Large Language Models

Overview

SCAR is an innovative data‑selection method that enhances instruction tuning for large language models. By leveraging style‑consistency‑aware response ranking, SCAR identifies and selects the most beneficial training data, ultimately improving model performance while slashing compute.
